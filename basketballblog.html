My First Year As Head Coach

Lessons Learned Being A Head Coach

by Ken Pagano

We all make mistakes. It's how we learn from them that make us better.

The following are a collection of memorable moments that I experienced in my first year as a head coach at Holy Rosary:

  • Sent a team e-mail to have all families attend the CYO mass.

    A day later, received an email from a mom stating that 'we're not cathoic and I think my son would feel uncomfortable. Is it okay if he can be excused?'

    LESSON LEARNED: Never assume all families on your CYO team are Catholic.
